# Adding Footer Links in Plone

## What are Footer Links?

Footer links are quick navigation links that appear at the bottom of every page on the website. They're useful for directing users to important resources or pages.

---

## Steps

### 1. Access Portal Settings

1. Click on **your name** in the bottom left corner (User Actions)
2. Select **Portal Settings**

### 2. Go to Categorized Footer Links

1. Navigate to **Categorized Footer Links**

### 3. Add a New Footer Link

1. Fill in:
   - **Title:** The text that will display in the footer
   - **URL:** The link destination

### 4. Prepare the URL

The URL must be hosted on the BCDSS website. If linking to a page or file:

1. Upload it to the appropriate folder in **medien-ordner** (media folder)
2. Copy the link
3. **Remove `/view` from the end** of the URL
4. Paste the cleaned URL into the footer link

**Example:**
- Full URL: `https://www.dependency.uni-bonn.de/en/medien-ordner/document/view`
- Correct URL: `https://www.dependency.uni-bonn.de/en/medien-ordner/document`

### 5. Save

Click **Save**. The footer link will now appear at the bottom of all pages.

---

## Tips

- Always remove the `/view` suffix from URLs
- Test the link after adding it to ensure it works correctly
