# Creating Business Cards in Plone

## What are Business Cards?

Business cards are simple profile cards that display a person's basic information and link to their full profile.

---

## Steps

### 1. Navigate to the Person Register

1. Go to **Contents**
2. Click the **Home icon** 🏠
3. Select **BCDSS**
4. Open **Person Register BCDSS Phase 2**

### 2. Add a New Business Card

1. Click **Add New**
2. Select **Person Business Card**

### 3. Fill in the Information

1. Enter the person's details
- Always remember to include copyright info if there is an image.
2. Click **Publish**

---
## Tips

- Keep information concise. Business cards should be quick reference cards
- Always include a link back to the full profile for more details
