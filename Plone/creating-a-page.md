# Creating a Page

This guide shows you how to create a new page from scratch in Plone.

---

## Before You Start

Are you logged in?

Make sure you're in the right section of the website. If you want to add a page under "Research → Projects," navigate there first.

> 💡 New pages are created inside whatever folder you're currently in. If you create a page in the wrong place, don't worry. You can move it later.

---

## Click "Add new..."

1. In the green toolbar, click **Add new...**
2. A dropdown menu will appear with several content types.
3. Click **tiles-seite**.

---

## Write Your Content

Below the title, you'll see the **text editor**. This is where you write the body of your page. It works similarly to a basic word processor.

You can:

- Type and format text (bold, italic, headings).
- Add links.
- Insert images.


---

## Save Your Page

See the green tick on the bottom of your screen. Move your mouse over it, find the option with 4 boxes in the menu that appears.
Click!

Your page is now created, but it is **not yet visible to the public**. It's saved as a draft. Publish it from "State" on the left.

---

- Don't forget: saving is not the same as publishing. Your page is still a draft until you publish.
