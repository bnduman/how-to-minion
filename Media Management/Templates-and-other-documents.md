## What You'll Find There

**Logos** — Official BCDSS and University of Bonn logos in various formats.

**Corporate Design** — Templates and guidelines for presentations, documents, and materials to keep everything looking consistent.

**Business Trips** — Forms and information for travel requests, expense claims, and daily allowance.

**Events** — Event planning guidelines and resources.

**Pregnancy Info** — Information and policies related to pregnancy.

**Absence** — Guidelines for vacation, sick leave, and time off requests.

**Funding Requests** — How to request funding for projects.

**Admin** — Information about personal data changes, onboarding, and offboarding.

**IT & Technical Issues** — Technical help and support resources. All roads lead to Danuja.

**Publications** — Guidelines and procedures for publications.

**Timesheet & Home Office** — Time tracking and remote work policies and resources.

**Onboarding** — Information for new staff members.

**Accident Reports** — How to report incidents or accidents.

**Holiday Requests** — Vacation request forms and procedures.

**Health & Well-Being** — Health and wellness resources.

**Zoom** — Video conferencing guidelines.

**UoB** — University of Bonn logos.
